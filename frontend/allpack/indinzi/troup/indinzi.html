Our Traditional Dance Styles

Intore Dance

The warrior's dance - celebrating Rwanda's bravery and heritage through powerful movements.

Umushayayo

Graceful movements that tell stories of Rwandan traditions and daily life.

Ikinyemera

Celebratory dance marking harvest seasons and community achievements.

Ritual Dances

Sacred movements connecting us to ancestors and spiritual traditions.

Ingoma

The drum dance - where rhythm and movement become one powerful expression.

Royal Dances

Elegant performances that once graced the courts of Rwandan kings.

Dance of Unity

Celebrating Rwanda's spirit of togetherness through synchronized movements.

Celebration Dance

Joyful expressions marking life's milestones in Rwandan culture.

Living History

Every step connects us to Rwanda's past while dancing toward the future.
